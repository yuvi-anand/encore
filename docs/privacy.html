Encore Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Encore (“we”, “the app”) helps you discover when your favorite artists are touring near you. This policy explains what we collect and why.

Information we collect

  • Account info: your email address (or your Spotify account identity if you sign in with Spotify).
  • Music data: when you connect Spotify, we read your top artists and followed artists to populate the app. We do not modify your Spotify account.
  • Location preferences: the home cities you enter. We convert these to approximate coordinates to measure distance to shows. We do not track your device’s real-time location.
  • Notification token: a push token so we can alert you about shows, if you enable notifications.
  • Artists and events you follow within the app.

How we use it

  • To show you upcoming concerts for the artists you follow, near the cities you choose.
  • To send optional notifications about new shows and reminders before events.

Third-party services

We share data only as needed to provide the service, with:

  • Spotify — to authenticate and read your library (per Spotify’s terms).
  • Ticketmaster — to look up concert and tour data.
  • Supabase — to store your account and app data securely.
  • OpenStreetMap (Nominatim) — to geocode the city names you enter.
  • Expo — to deliver push notifications.

We do not sell your personal data.

Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Manage account → Delete Account. This removes your profile, followed artists, and preferences from our database.
