Common questions

My shows aren’t loading. Pull down on the Feed to refresh, and make sure you’ve added at least one home city in Settings.

Connecting Spotify isn’t working. Make sure you’re on the latest version and try again from Settings → Connected Accounts.

How do I delete my account? Go to Settings → Manage account → Delete Account.
